How do we work?

Our experienced team delivers reliable, on-time service across aircon, electrical, painting, and more—all with transparent pricing and quality workmanship. As a trusted all-in-one contractor in Singapore, we handle everything from repairs to renovations with care, professionalism, and strong aftercare. Most of our clients come through referrals—and we’re proud of that.

Step 01

Free Site Inspection

We visit your property, photograph problem areas, and assess damage severity. You explain what’s failing, and we identify what needs fixing beyond just painting. Our schedule fits your availability – evenings and weekends work.

Step 02

Itemized Quote

You receive a breakdown listing rooms, wall repairs, ceiling work, coat numbers, paint brands and grades, anti-mould treatments, and timeline. We provide pricing transparently, upfront, so you’ll know if we’re including primer and what warranty covers.

Step 03

Surface Preparation

Our crew scrapes loose paint, fills cracks, sands rough patches, cleans mould with treatment solutions, and applies primer where needed. Your furniture gets wrapped, and floors and fixtures get protected. We know proper prep takes time, but we don’t skip it.

Step 04

Paint Application

Multiple coats go on with drying time between layers, so we check for missed spots, drips, and uneven coverage before calling a wall finished. Low-VOC formulas mean minimal smell – quicker to become “ready to live.” Also, work happens room by room.

Step 05

Final Walkthrough

We walk through together. You point out anything needing touch-ups, and we handle corrections immediately. Once you approve, we clean thoroughly, remove all protection materials, and hand over warranty documentation.

The promises we make and always keep

Promise 01

No compromise on quality, whatsoever!

We only buy Nippon and Dulux paints with anti-mould additives and low-VOC formulas. We don’t work with cheap alternatives or watered-down coats that fade in six months. Weather-resistant finishes come with every job, and your walls get 5-10 years of protection (instead of just coverage)

Promise 02

You’ll get people who know what they’re doing

Old HDB blocks develop hairline cracks, concrete spalling, and moisture ruins paint within months if you skip proper prep. That’s why we’ve hired painters with at least 5+ years of experience handling buildings with real damage. They know where to scrape, seal, and which primers stop problems from returning.

Promise 03

Giving it back to you as “ready-to-live

We cover floors, mask electrical outlets, and remove dust sheets at day’s end. Furniture gets wrapped, low-VOC formulas dry without that choking smell, and your home doesn’t become a construction zone. We arrive on time, work quietly, and hand over rooms you can use immediately.
